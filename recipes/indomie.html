NIGERIAN INDOMIE

DESCRIPTION

Indomie is a brand of instant noodles produced by the indonesian company (indo food). Indo food itself is the largest indo food producer in the world with 16 factories. Over 15 billion packets of indomie are produced annually. Indomie is also exported to more than 90 countries around the world. Indofood key export market are:

  • Australia
  • New Zealand
  • Iraq
  • Papua, New Guinea
  • Hong Kong
  • Canada
  • Saudi Arabia, e.t.c

Indomie is a unique brand of instant noodles, loved by a majority of nigerians. it has grown to become a household name in nigeria owing to it delightful taste, and wholesomeness. Transcending across all ages groups, the brand has manage to win over the heart and taste palates of both the young and old. Being in nigeria for last 31 years, indomie has made a remarkable impact on the country's culinary landscape.

INGREDIENTS

  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Sliced Onions
  • Fresh papper
  • Eggs
  • Some Vegetable Oil

COOKING-STEPS

  1. Pieces the indomie in a bowl and pour boiled water into it. Allowing it for about 5 minutes for the noodles to get soft.
  2. Set your frying pan on the heat, add Sliced Tomatoes, Onions, Fresh papper, and fry for about five minutes as well.
  3. Drain water from the noodles, after 4 minutes add the fellow-come spices while still in bowl, the noodles will be both soft and hot.
  4. Break the two eggs into thesame bowl containing the noodles and stir together, then transfer to frying pan.
    The frying pan contain the fried Tomatoes, Onions, and pappers, cook and stir for about 5 minutes, and you are done.
